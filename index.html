Bros of Mayhem

Basic Rules

1. All task force members are expected to maintain a 70% participation rate and must deploy and attack will all troops until the base or your troops are destroyed during an attack.

- Stats can be monitored through the Task Force Command window, going to the My Task Force tab, clicking on the Stats button, clicking on the Member Stats button, and using the left arrow near the top to go to the Grand Total Page.

- Under Grand Total in Member Stats, if your “Attacks missed” plus your “Attacks done” are less than 10, you can miss up to 2 attacks. Upon missing your 3rd, you will be kicked. This allows new members some flexibility if they miss their first couple attacks. After that, you are subject to the 70% rule mentioned above.

2. If you get promoted to an Officer or Leader, do not use intel for Sabotage within Operations. You will get 1 warning, and then will be demoted to the point you are not able to use intel.

3. Do not express prejudicial words towards another member of the Task Force. If done, you will get 1 warning, and if done again, you will be kicked.
